Where the Name Comes From

Built from two words that are usually on opposite sides.

Tenallo blends tenant and landlord into one word — because that's the actual job. Most property management treats those two relationships as a tug-of-war. We built Tenallo on the idea that they don't have to be.

When a tenant feels heard and a landlord feels protected, neither side is fighting the other — they're both just living their lives. That's the outcome we manage toward, on every tier of service.

For Landlords

Protected, not policing

Your asset is cared for without you having to be the bad guy every time something needs attention.

For Tenants

Heard, not handled

Requests get a real response, not a runaround — because a respected tenant is a long-term tenant.
